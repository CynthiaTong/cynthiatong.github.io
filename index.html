Lights of Constellations

Dec, 15

"Lights of Constellation" is a project I did for the Interactive Media showcase in December, 2015 at NYU Abu Dhabi. The idea is to let the computer communicate with a physical object. I mappped the night sky chart on a computer screen to a physical board of LEDs, and coded the serial communication between Processing programs and an Arduino microcontroller.

Art & Design

GeoMatrix

May, 16

GeoMatrix is a sculpture project I created for the end-of-semester 3D show at NYU Abu Dhabi in May, 2016. The structure is built with roughly 50 wooden sticks. Each 35cm long, 3cm by 3cm thick. They are drilled and joined together by nails. The joints are not hidden, as they represent the "raw" nature of carpentry and art making.
